How to play

    You have a Player and Enemies (Bugs). Each time your player reaches the water (at the top) without colliding into any enemies, you score 10 points.
    The player has 3 lives (could die 3 times) before the game is over.
    The goal is to win with the highest score!

  • press space _ or enter ↵: restart the game
  • press left arrow ←: move Player to the left
  • press right arrow →: move Player to the right
  • press up arrow↑: move Player up
  • press down arrow ↓: move Player down
