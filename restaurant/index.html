Fresh - Healthy - Delicious

Hello, we are Mint & Basil! We are built on passion for spreading the vision of peace and love in the world by enjoying great food without the sacrificing of animals. Our goal is to help more people enjoy vegan/vegetarian meals that are not only healthy but also full of flavor and nutrition. Our Asian dishes are from Vietnamese, Thai, and Malaysian influences.

Fresh

All our vegetables are fresh, and local. We carefully select our vegetables to make sure they are always fresh and made ready to order.

Healthy

We do not use MSG. Our food is Good for your health, the environment, and it also tastes better!

Delicious

We work with the best chefs and recipes to ensure that you're 100% happy. You will love our signature Autumn Rolls, Rock ‘n Roll “Beef”, and our Signature Curry. On a cold day, you and a partner can enjoy our Mushroom Hotpot with noodles and fresh vegetables!
