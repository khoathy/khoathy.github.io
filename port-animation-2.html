Mom and I

About the Project

I took this animation project from concept to finished work totally on my own, and everything in the animation was illustrated by me. I used my own hand-drawn sketches, hand-written text, watercolor wash, acrylic paint, digitally rendered textures, in combination with digital painting to create digital mixed media paintings. → View these illustrations

I then animated each scene, added sound effects, and put everything together as an e-greeting card in Adobe After Effects.

The project is filled with love and sweet memories with my Mom.

Song : "How sweet it is to be loved by you"
